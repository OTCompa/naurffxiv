### P2: Omega-M/F
- [Phase 2 Toolbox](https://ff14.toolboxgaming.space/?id=324285310725761&preview=1)
- Group 1 on Male, Group 2 on Female
- Conga: H1 R1 M1 MT OT M2 R2 H2
- Western most shape goes left facing eye, eastern most shape goes right facing eye
- Stack Flex Priorities: Northern most stack flexes if same side | East group Flexes South if Mid Glitch
